---
title: "Noodles teriyaki "
description: A plate of noodles with teriyaki sauce.
image: /images/noodles-teriyaki.jpg
ingredients:
  - Chicken, rice noodles, paprika, onion, brocoli, vegetables as you want
steps:
  - Cut the vegetables and make them on a pan.
  - Cut the chicken and make it on a separate pan.
  - Make the noodles 5 minutes on the boiling water
  - When the vegetables are done put teriyaki sauce and then throw the chicken
    and the noodles. Mix it and done
tag: Chicken, Noodles, Asian food
