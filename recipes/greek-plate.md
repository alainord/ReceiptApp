# Greek dish tray bake

Really lekker tray bake with chicken and lot of vegetables.

![Dish image](/images/Greek-traybake.jpg)

## Ingredients

**Protain base**
- Chicken

**Vegetables**
- Zucchini
- Yellow and red paprika
- Red onion
- Olives
- More vegetables as the taste of the cooker

**Others**
- Potatos
- Garlic
- Rosermarie
- Oregane
- Thyme
- Dille
- Lemon
- Olive oil
- Feta cheese

## Steps

1. Cut the chicken in stripes, put it in a bowl and marinate with olive oil, lemon zest.garlic and from each of spice a tsbp mix it and keep for at least 30 minutes.
2. Cut the onion paprika zucchini in stripes and put the spices on it with olive oil 
3. Cut the potatos in half (they need to be small, or also cubes) and mix with olive oil and a lot of garlic ad oregane.
4.  Put everything on a traybake, potatos and chicken in one traybake and vegetables in the other.
5 Let them in the oven on 180ยบ With vegetables first on top, and after 15 minutes turn them, put the olives. Leave them for another 10-15 minutes.
6. Place everything on plates and put the feta chees with olive oils.
7. Take care with the pepper and salt bevause feta cheese is salty.
