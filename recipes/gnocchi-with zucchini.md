# Red Pesto Chicken & Zucchini Gnocchi Bake

A simple, comforting oven-baked gnocchi dish with chicken, zucchini, and creamy red pesto. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

![Dish image](/images/IMAGE_NAME.jpg)

## Ingredients

**Protein & Base**
- ~500 g chicken breast (cubed)
- 2 packages gnocchi (for 4 people)
- 1 jar red pesto

**Vegetables**
- 2 zucchinis (sliced or cubed)

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to **200Â°C**.
2. Place the gnocchi, cubed chicken, and sliced zucchini on a baking tray.
3. Add the **red pesto** and mix everything until well coated.
4. Bake for **10 minutes**.
5. Remove the tray and mix again.
6. Bake for another **10 minutes**.
7. Serve hot.
