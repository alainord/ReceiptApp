# Bacon Jam

A sweet–savory bacon spread with caramelized onions, garlic, balsamic vinegar and a hint of coffee or whisky. Perfect for burgers, sandwiches, toast, or cheese boards.

![Dish image](/images/bacon-jam.jpg)

## Ingredients

**Bacon & Base**
- 150 g bacon, chopped
- 1 tbsp white sugar
- 1 tbsp honey (or syrup)
- 1 tbsp strong coffee or 5 ml whisky
- 1–2 tbsp water, if the mixture becomes too dry
- ½ tsp sweet paprika (optional)

**Vegetables**
- 1 large onion, caramelized
- 1 clove garlic, minced or garlic paste

## Steps

1. Thinly slice the onion and caramelize it over medium-low heat until soft and golden.
2. Cook the bacon until crispy but not burnt. Keep all the fat in 
3. Add the caramelized onion to the bacon and sauté together for 1–2 minutes.
4. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
5. Mix in sugar, honey, and balsamic vinegar.
6. Add the coffee (or whisky) and let it bubble briefly.
7. Simmer on low heat until thick and jam-like, adding small amounts of water if needed.
8. Adjust sweetness or acidity to taste.
9. Let cool slightly; it will thicken as it cools.
